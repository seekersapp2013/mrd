Making Your Visit to MRD Easy

Thank you for choosing us for your imaging and laboratory needs. We will do our best to make your experience convenient, comfortable and pleasant.

PreCheck

Virtually register for your appointment
from outside of our centers.

Allow the registration process to occur prior
to your arrival at any of our centers.
PreCheck will vary by patient depending on the
information that we have on file for you.
Please know that we have created a secure system
of exchanging information that ensures we are keeping
your personal information private.

Virtual Check-in

Take advantage of virtual check-in and practice
social distancing while waiting outside of the center.

Prior to your appointment, you should receive
a text message from us. Upon arrival at our center,
text (or call if you prefer) the number that texted
you ahead of time. If you didnâ€™t receive a text,
we have the number on display outside of our centers.

Get Your Results

Get reports, view images, and
download your medical information.

Use our online Connect Patient Portal to
access your exam results and images. We will
provide you with an access code that can be used
to quickly navigate within our portal to your recent
exam, or you can set up a portal account to have
access to your complete exam history.

Leave a Review

After your visit, you will receive
a text from us asking you to rate our service.

30 minutes after your visit to our center,
you will receive a text asking you to rate
our service via Facebook or Google. We ask
that you provide feedback on your overall
service experience.

Your service experience with us matters!
