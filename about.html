About Us

Medicaid Radio-diagnostics has been serving Abuja and its environs since 2009. We are never content with the status quo, we continue to add services, staff, programs and technology to benefit our patients and partners. From our state-of-the-art Molecular Lab with its digital communications to our holistic wellness program to our comprehensive cancer screening programs.
